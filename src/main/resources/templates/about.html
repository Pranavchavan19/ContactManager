About Smart Contact Manager

Welcome to your smart solution for managing contacts efficiently.

Smart Contact Manager is designed to simplify your life by keeping your contacts organized, secure, and accessible anytime. Built using modern technologies like Spring Boot, MongoDB, and React, this tool ensures a smooth and reliable experience.

With features like user authentication, cloud image storage (Cloudinary), and email integration, you can fully manage personal or business contacts with ease.

  • Secure login and registration (OAuth support)
  • Beautiful, mobile-responsive UI
  • Store, edit, and delete contacts easily
  • Integrated cloud image uploads
  • Mail sending capabilities
